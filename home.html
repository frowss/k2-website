"K2 isn't about climbing a mountain; it's about surviving it." -Steve House (American alpinist)

K2, or Mount Godwin-Austen, is well-known as the 2nd highest mountain in the world. Often dubbed "The Savage Mountain", it is infamous for its steep, pyramid-like slopes, unpredictable weather, and technical challenges that make it one of the most dangerous and difficult mountains to climb.
