maps ―

Over the years, many detailed maps and routes have been established on K2. Using these maps, and information gathered from other climbers, future mountaineers can carefully plan out their expeditions on the dangerous slopes of K2.

main routes ―

Over the years, climbers have established and pioneered specific routes to summit K2. The map shown contains the notable routes used by mountaineers over the years, marked by the coloured lines:
Route A: West Ridge
Route B: West Face
Route C: Southwest Pillar
Route D: South Face
Route E: South-southeast Spur
Route F: Abruzzi Spur
The Abruzzi Spur is the most popular route, with around 75% successful climbs using it due to its more straight-forward path compared to other routes.
